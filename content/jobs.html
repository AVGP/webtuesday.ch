--- title: Jobs ---

We recommend the following job platforms...

freshjobs.ch

freshjobs.ch is a platform for listing Internet-specific jobs e.g. webdesigner, Frontend Engineer, PHP developer etc. Employers pay a small fee to list jobs and potential candidates get to browse for free.

skillbill.ch

skillbill.ch is not your typical job platform. Instead candidates can present themselves anonymously, based on their skillset, for potential employers to discover them based on their needs.
