--- date: 2010-11-09 title: November 9, 2010 kind: meeting abstract: Performance and stability of Amazee and other PHP platforms ---

Location: Amazee

Presentation

Slides: http://www.slideshare.net/AmazeeAG/php-high-availability-high-performance

Agenda

Michael Schmid will talk about, how Amazee is using different technologies and softwares (Memcache, GlusterFS, eAccelerator, Nginx/Apache, Amazon EC2/RDS) to improve performance and stability of there PHP platform. An overview of the last one year, what we are still using and what we have rejected, because it isn't working.
