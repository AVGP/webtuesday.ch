We start at 19:30 and doors open at 19:00 at the Swiss television (Fernsehstrasse 1-4, Zurich), audience entrance. Use the tram 11 until the station "Fernsehstudio".

Mobile Apps vs. Mobile Web - The final showdown

  • Mobile web applications are written by people who are afraid of learning a real programming language.
  • Native apps are a walled garden with lots of lock-in.
  • Mobile web applications are slow, ugly and buggy.
  • Native apps provide inconsistent interfaces across platforms.

Are you in to discuss any of these points? Harry Fuecks and Patrice Neff will lead a discussion and hopefully show solutions as well.
