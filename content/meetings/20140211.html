Continuous Integration Testing with the Selenium Ecosystem

Selenium is probably the best-known web testing framework. David will give an introduction into and overview of the ecosystem of tools that has grown up around it, and describe a cloud-based CI solution using Selenium Builder, GitHub, Sauce On Demand and Travis.

David has been working for Sauce Labs for the past 3.5 years. He is the main developer of Selenium Builder.
